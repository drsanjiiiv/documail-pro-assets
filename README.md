# DocuMail Pro 🚀

A modern, highly intuitive Document Automation and Mail Merge workspace for Google Sheets. DocuMail Pro completely replaces clunky, multi-step wizards with a beautiful, fast sidebar interface right inside your spreadsheet.

As seen in **"Screenshot-DocuMail-Pro.png"**, the add-on features a clean, card-based management console for your automated jobs.

---

## ✨ Key Differentiators & Features

Unlike traditional merge tools, DocuMail Pro gives you complete control over your workflow instantly from the sidebar:

* **Three Powerful Template Modes:**
  * 📄 **PDF or DOC Only:** Generate formatted documents and track file IDs/URLs seamlessly.
  * ✉️ **Email Only:** Execute high-speed personalized email merges directly via Gmail.
  * 🔄 **PDF or DOC & Email:** Combine both to generate custom attachments and email them simultaneously.
* **On-Demand Action Suite:** Every template card comes equipped with dedicated buttons to **Edit** layouts, **Preview** outputs, **Run** the merge automation instantly, or **Delete** the configuration.
* **Smart Sheet Interoperability:** Automatically appends and tracks data destinations like recipient emails, document merge statuses, and file URLs.

## 🛡️ Privacy & Security
DocuMail Pro is designed for corporate and enterprise-grade privacy. All computations, document builds, and email processing occur directly inside your native Google Workspace environment. We never store, collect, or transmit your private business data to external servers.
